YOLOv8-SIEMF: A Visual Model Based on Sub-models Integral Evaluation and Multi-objective Filter

Donghao Cao, Fuchen Huang, Zhengxuan Wei, Jingyi Zhu, Jingsi Lyu

Under Review

To address missed detection and misdetection in tea bud recognition under complex environments, this paper proposes YOLOv8-SIEMF, integrating Sub-models Integral Evaluation (SIE) and Multi-objective Filtering (MF). A hierarchical detection framework processes diverse resolution inputs, while an evaluation mechanism fuses sub-model outputs by confidence, box overlap, and image sharpness. The multi-objective filtering module enhances sensitivity to multi-target clusters, reducing redundant detection. Experiments on a self-built dataset demonstrate superior precision and recall over mainstream YOLOv8 variants.

Published

The Position Visualization Modeling of Mechanical Product Assembly Line Based on Equidistant Helix

Donghao Cao, Fuchen Huang, Ziyi Wang, Kui Duanmu

AIVRV, 2024

This study presents a uniformly spaced recursive model to determine position and velocity of mechanical products at various locations and time intervals on an assembly line. Assuming constant speed, initial product positions are calculated by arc length at different intervals. A node position model based on the Cosine Rule recursively determines the front handle center of each subsequent product relative to the preceding product's rear handle center, enabling precise visualization and optimization of assembly line dynamics.
